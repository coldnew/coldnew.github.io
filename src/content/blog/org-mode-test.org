* Introduction

Welcome to my blog! This post is written in **org-mode** format.

**Why org-mode?**

- Keep your notes in plain text
- Git-friendly (no conversion needed)
- Powerful formatting with plain text

* Features

Org-mode supports:

1. *Bold text*
2. /Italic text/
3. =Code=
4. [[https://example.com][Links]]
